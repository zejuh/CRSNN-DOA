Research Findings

What the benchmark says.

The underlying benchmark compares SNN, ANN, and classical DOA models under the same 4-microphone synthetic spatialization setup. The demo menu exposes the selected checkpoints directly, while the summary below shows the main research takeaways.

Best overall robustness: ConvRecSNN is the strongest noisy-condition model.
Best efficiency trade-off: FlatLIFSNN keeps much lower SynOps while staying competitive.
Classical baseline: GCC-PHAT + least-squares is useful as a reference, but weaker than the best SNN.
Model selection note: clean-validation lambda is not always the same as noisy-test best lambda.
